Want to join us?

I am currently looking for a postdoctoral scholar to develop and implement a new parameterization for upper-ocean mixing in a next-generation Earth System Model (Prospective Start Date: Fall 2021). The scholar will be involved with all aspects of parameterizations development, with particular focus on including surface wave effects in the mixing parameterization and improving turbulence closure assumptions. The postdoc will carry out numerical simulations (LES) of idealized boundary layers, use these LES to build a physically-consistent parameterization for upper ocean mixing, implement this parameterization in an Earth System Model, and assess the impact of the new parametrization on this model. Please direct any inquiries to me via email.
